DocScheduler Help Center

Everything you need to know about using our platform

Getting Started

Creating an Account

To use DocScheduler's features, you'll need to create an account first:

  1. Click on "Log In" in the navigation bar
  2. Select "Register here" at the bottom of the login form
  3. Fill in your personal information
  4. Click "Register" to create your account

Once registered, you'll have access to all patient features including appointment scheduling and health record management.

Logging In

If you already have an account:

  1. Click "Log In" in the top navigation bar
  2. Enter your email address and password
  3. Click the "Log In" button

If you've forgotten your password, please contact support for assistance with resetting it.

Finding Doctors

Using the Interactive Map

Our interactive map helps you find healthcare facilities near you:

  1. Navigate to the homepage and scroll down to the map section
  2. Click on any marker to view details about that hospital
  3. View available specialties and doctors at that location
  4. Click "Schedule Appointment" to book with a doctor at that facility

The map shows all partner hospitals in your region with real-time information about available specialists.

Scheduling Appointments

How to Book an Appointment

Booking an appointment is simple and straightforward:

  1. Click on "Add appointment" in the navigation bar or click "Schedule Appointment" from the map
  2. Select your preferred hospital from the dropdown menu
  3. Choose a doctor from the available specialists
  4. Select a date on the calendar (green dates indicate full availability)
  5. Choose an available time slot (shown in green)
  6. Enter the reason for your visit
  7. Click "Schedule Appointment" to confirm

The color-coding system on the calendar helps you quickly identify availability:

  • ◉ Green: All slots available
  • ◉ Yellow: Partially booked
  • ◉ Red: Fully booked

Managing Your Health Records

Viewing Your Health Record

Access your complete medical information:

  1. Click on "My Records" in the navigation menu
  2. View your personal information, medical history, and appointments
  3. Review past appointments and their findings

Your health record includes personal details, allergies, medical conditions, and a comprehensive appointment history.

Updating Your Profile

Keep your information current:

  1. Go to "My Records"
  2. Click "Edit Profile" in the top right corner
  3. Update your personal information, allergies, or other details
  4. Click "Save Changes" when finished

Maintaining accurate information ensures your healthcare providers have the most current data for your care.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment?
Currently, to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please contact the healthcare facility directly. We're working on adding self-service rescheduling features in the near future.
What if I can't find my preferred doctor?
If you can't find a specific doctor, they may not be registered in our system yet. You can filter doctors by specialty and hospital to find alternatives, or contact us to request adding a particular healthcare provider.
Is my medical information secure?
Yes, we take data security very seriously. All your medical information is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with healthcare data protection regulations and only authorized personnel can access your information.
Can I book appointments for family members?
Currently, each user needs their own account. We're working on a family management feature that will allow you to book and manage appointments for family members from a single account.
What do I do if I miss my appointment?
If you miss your appointment, please contact the healthcare facility directly to explain the situation and reschedule. Some facilities may have policies regarding missed appointments, including possible fees.
